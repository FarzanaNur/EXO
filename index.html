EXO

SM ENTERTAINMENT

EXO is a name inspired by EXOPLANET, which means a planet outside the solar system, and implicitly means a new star from an unknown world.

SUB-UNITS

EXO-K

 2012-2014

EXO-K is a sub-group of EXO;consists of: Suho, Baekhyun, Chanyeol, D.O, Kai, and Sehun. This unit is currently inactive.

EXO-M

 2012-2014

EXO-M is the Chinese subgroup of EXO ; consisted of: Kris, Xiumin, Lay, Chen, Luhan, and Tao. This unit hasn’t been active since the departure of Kris, Luhan, and Tao in 2016.

EXO-CBX

 2016-NOW

EXO-CBX is a 3-member sub-unit of EXO;consists of Xiumin, Baekhyun, and Chen under INB100 since january,2024.The group debuted on October 31, 2016 under SM Entertainment.

EXO-SC

 2019-NOW

EXO-SC is the 2nd sub-unit of EXO;consists of Chanyeol and Sehun. They officially debuted on July 22, 2019, with “What a Life” EP under SM Entertainment .
